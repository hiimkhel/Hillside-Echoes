Iloilo Provincial Government launches teen center version 2.0

BY PRECIOUS COLEEN CUBIN

The Iloilo Provincial Government held a groundbreaking ceremony of the two-storey teen center version 2.0 building at Alimodian National Comprehensive High School.

The teen center is the flagship project of the Iloilo Provincial Government under the mantra, MoRProGRes Iloilo or A Movement for a Robust, Progressive, Globally Competitive and Resilient Province of Iloilo.

“It will be a haven where they can develop their personality, talents, skills, and a sanctuary to enhance their well-being, and prepare themselves for the challenges and triumphs of the future,” Atty. Ruel Von Superio, Provincial Population Officer, said during the groundbreaking ceremony.

Governor Athur Defensor Jr. also added that building teen centers at schools provides an avenue for youths to learn values and knowledge which can’t be learned inside the classroom.

Moreover, Defensor allocated funds to renovate the classrooms of the school under the BES Probins or the Bulig Eskwela sang Probinsya program.

The groundbreaking ceremony was attended by Governor Arthur Defensor Jr., Mayor Ian Kenneth Alfeche of Alimodian, Mayor Lee Ann Debuque, who represented the League of Municipalities of the Philippines, Provincial Population Officer Atty. Ruel Von Superio, Provincial Engineer Romeo Andig, ANCHS Principal Romar C. Cubin, and other provincial and local officials.
